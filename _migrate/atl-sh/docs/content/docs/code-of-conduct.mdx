## Our Pledge

The atl.sh pubnix is a shared space. We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Be collaborative and helpful
- Use resources responsibly
- Report problems to admins

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Attempting to access other users' files or accounts
- Cryptocurrency mining or resource abuse
- Network abuse (port scanning, DoS, spam)
- Hosting illegal content
- Circumventing resource limits

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

1. Warning
2. Temporary suspension
3. Permanent ban

## Contact

Report issues to `admin@allthingslinux.org` or on IRC `#support`.
